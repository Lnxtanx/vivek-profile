Gurukul Kangri Vishwavidyalaya

University Details

Gurukul Kangri Vishwavidyalaya, also known as Gurukul Kangri University, is a renowned higher education institution located in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India. Here are some key details about the university:

Overview

- Founded: March 4, 1902, by Swami Shraddhanandaji.
- Type: Public Deemed University.
- Location: Haridwar, Uttarakhand, on the banks of the Ganges River, about 200 km from New Delhi.
- Campus Size: 205 acres.
- Accreditation: Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade.

Academic Programs

Gurukul Kangri University offers a wide range of programs across various disciplines, including:

  • Engineering: B.Tech in various specializations.
  • Science and Technology: B.Sc, M.Sc, and Ph.D. programs.
  • Commerce and Management: BBA, MBA, and other management courses.
  • Humanities and Social Sciences: BA, MA, and other humanities programs.
  • Vedic Sciences: Courses in Vedic literature, Indian philosophy, and culture.

Facilities and Services

- Library: Well-equipped with a vast collection of books and journals.
- Placement Cell: Provides placement assistance to students with top recruiters like TCS, HCL, Asian Paints, and Infosys.
- Research: Strong emphasis on research in both modern and Vedic sciences.
- Sports and Extracurricular Activities: Various sports facilities and extracurricular activities to promote holistic development.

Notable Features

- Historical Significance: Established to revive the ancient Indian Gurukula system of education.
- Distinguished Guests: The university has hosted many prominent figures, including Mahatma Gandhi, Pt. Madan Mohan Malaviya, and Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan.
- Women's Education: The Kanya Gurukula Campus in Haridwar was established in 1993 to promote women's education.

Gurukul Kangri University is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering an environment of learning and research. It continues to uphold the vision of its founder, Swami Shraddhanandaji, by offering a blend of traditional and modern education.
